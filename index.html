About

An open-source database of cardiovascular models

What it is

A library of computational models of normal and diseased human cardiovascular models.

Who it is for

Academic, government and industry researchers. Our models can be used to verify computational methods for fluid and solid mechanics.

Our sponsors

The National Institutes of Health (NIH) under the direction of the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (contract HHSN268201100035C) and the NIH National Library of Medicine (grant 5R01LM013120).

SimVascular-ready projects

SimVascular is an open-source software providing a complete pipeline from medical image data segmentation to patient specific blood flow simulation and analysis. This repository features projects that are compatible with SimVascular containing medical images, segmentation files, 3D models and meshes, and simulation files. Need help getting started with SimVascular? Visit the Getting started guide or the Forum for general issues.
