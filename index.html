About

An open-source database of cardiovascular models

What it is

A library of computational models of normal and diseased human cardiovascular models.

Who it is for

Academic, government and industry researchers. Our models can be used to verify computational methods for fluid and solid mechanics.

Our sponsors

The National Institutes of Health under the direction of the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (Contract HHSN268201100035C).

SimVascular-ready projects

SimVascular is an open-source software providing a complete pipeline from medical image data segmentation to spatient specific blood flow simulation and analysis. This repository features complete SimVascular projects, containing medical images, segmentation files, 3D models and meshes, and simulation files.
